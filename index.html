My Style Guide

Colors

Charcoal
#2D2E30

Charcoal shall be used for content backgrounds and for text on light backgrounds.

Olive
#50A38C

Olive (and variations in its saturation and lightness) shall be used for content backgrounds and headings.

Mint
#69F0CA

Mint (and variations in its saturation and lightness) shall be used for primary buttons and navigation.

Pumpkin
#F0A85D

Pumpkin (and variations in its saturation and lightness) shall be used for secondary buttons and links.

Navy
#2D2E30

Navy shall be used for content backgrounds.

Periwinkle
#5172F0

Periwinkle shall be used for content accents and links.

Text

Page Titles

CSS styles:

h1 {
  background-color: #50A38C;
  color: white;
  font-family: Bungee Outline;
  font-size: 92px;
  font-weight: 700;
  margin: 12px;
  max-width: 70%;
  padding: 23px;
  text-align: center;
}

Main Section Headings

CSS styles:
